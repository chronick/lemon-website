Flash Eurorack firmware via WebUSB

WebUSB is not available in this browser. Use Chrome or Edge.

Flash

How to flash

  1. Select a firmware above
  2. Put your patch.init() in DFU mode: hold BOOT + press RESET
  3. Click Connect and select the STM32 device
  4. Click Flash and wait for completion
  5. Press RESET to start your new firmware
